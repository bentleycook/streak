Setting up Streak

To set up habit tracking, simply press the Save button on the settings pop-up. Mark off the days that you have completed the the streak on the calendar, and the front of the Trello card will track your progress.

Streak will show a yellow background if you are in danger of losing your streak today, and green if you have an ongoing streak.

Streak Settings

If you want to track a habit that you only do a couple of times a week, you can choose the minimum number of days you need to complete your habit per week with the Days in week setting. If you complete your streak more than the minimum number of days you specify, it still will be counted towards your streak!

You can also customise whether your week starts on a Sunday or a Monday.

Deleting your streak

Your streak will be deleted if you delete the card that it is tracked on, or if you press the Delete button. This is an irreversible action.
