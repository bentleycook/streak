Streak Privacy Policy

This policy applies to all information collected or submitted on the Streak Power-up for Trello.

Information we collect

No information is stored or collected by Streak.

When using the power-up, data is collected and stored in the Trello card itself.

This data is limited to the two settings ("Days in week" and "First day of week") and the list of days that you have completed a habit.

Your consent

By using the Streak Trello Power-up, you consent to our privacy policy.

Contact us

For questions regarding this privacy policy, please email contact@emgoto.com.
